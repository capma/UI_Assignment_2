Chapter 01: Don't make me think

Next »

  • Don’t Make Me (or Anyone) Think

    • If you make users think, you make them frustrated.

    • Users don’t want to treat your website like some kind of puzzle.

    • They want to know what they should do instantly.

    • The more you make people think, the more they head somewhere else to get the job done.

  • Self-evident

    • The goal is whenever average users open each page they should be able to know at a glance what it’s for.

  • Self-explanatory

    • Occasionally, a website takes a little thought for average users to “get it”.

      In that case, strive to make it self-explanatory via well-chosen names, the small amounts of carefully crafted text, the layout of the page.

      Ensure everything can be figured out at a glance.
