Chapter 04: Users like mindless choices

« Previous Next »

The number of clicks actually doesn't matter if no thought goes behind each click, no uncertainty exists regarding where each click will lead.

A rule could be: "three mindless, unambiguous clicks equal one thoughtful click."

Therefore, when presenting a difficult choice to the user, divide it into simple clicks.
