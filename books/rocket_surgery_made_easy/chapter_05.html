Chapter 05: Who to Test with and How to Find them?

« Previous Next »

Who do you test with?

You should recruit a person who is able to express thoughts and feelings easily and clearly.

It's good enough to have 3 users in each round of testing for a number of reasons:

  • The first three users are very likely to encounter many of the most critical issues.

  • Finding three participants is easier than finding more.

  • Testing with 3 users makes it easier to do more rounds.

Where do you find them?

Base on the purpose of the website, you can find different kind of participants we want.

For example:

  • If you want to test with senior citizens, then consider senior centers, libraries, and church groups

  • If you need “just anyone,” then consider friends, family, and neighbors.

  • Testing with your team member is tempting. But chances are they know too much about your site.
