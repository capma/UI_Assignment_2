Chapter 01: What is "Do-It-Yourself Usability Testing"

Next »

"Usability testing" is watching other people try to use your website with the intention of:

  • making it convenient for people to use or

  • proving that it is easy to use

The purpose of "usability testing" is to prove something, that is we have to be very careful, or the result won't be trustworthy.

On the other hand, the purpose of the "Do-it-yourself usability testing" is not to prove anything, it enables you, as a website developer, to improve what you're building.

The "Do-it-yourself usability testing" always works because:

  • All sites have issues.

  • Most of the serious problems tend to be easy to find and fix.

  • Watching other users using your website makes you a better designer.
